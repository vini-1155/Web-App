How do you take measurements?
Yes, we do provide measurement service at home for Women's only. In case you have a perfect fitting reference, you can hand it over to the pickup executives. For Men's, we mandatorily need a reference clothing for the measurement purpose.
Do you sell readymade outfits?
No. We don't sell off-the-rack, standard-sized clothing. What makes us unique is that every garment we ship is tailored specially to your unique measurements.
I have an outfit idea. Can you bring it to life?
Yes! You can explain your idea to our designer or show us a Pinterest reference. We'll take care of the rest.
What about fabric?
If you're choosing styles from our in-house fashion labels, the fabric is included. If you're customising with Binks, we can either pick up your fabric (if you have it already) or source it for you (at no extra charge).
Does Threads Tailor provide alteration of the delivered order?
Yes, a reverse pickup will be attempted once the alteration request is raised.
